Railway traffic
[yearly plans map service](https://rata.digitraffic.fi/vuosisuunnitelmat/) will
be discontinued on June 18, 2024.

Data will be still available via
[Jeti-API](https://rata.digitraffic.fi/jeti-api/) service.
